WELCOME TO SCHULZE PROJECTS & ELECTRICAL

We are a locally owned and operated contracting business servicing South West Brisbane with over 30 years experience.

Fully licensed and insured we offer all aspects of residential, commercial and industrial electrical work.

We have built a solid reputation based on exceptional customer relationships, personalized and responsive service, attention to detail and commitment to ensure the highest possible level of quality work.

We would love the opportunity to assist you or your business with your electrical needs. Be it a big or small job, we are always happy to help. We understand that electrical problems can occur at any time of the day or night and for this reason we are available 24/7 for emergencies.

Schulze Projects & Electrical has all your electrical needs covered.
